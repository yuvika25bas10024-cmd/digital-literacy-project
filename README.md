 **PROJECT OVERVIEW**
 
This project serves as a comprehensive Digital Literacy Portfolio, designed to demonstrate the essential skills required for a Student Digital Ambassador. In the modern academic and professional landscape, technical proficiency must be balanced with a responsible and professional online presence. Throughout this assignment, I have completed five distinct tasks mapping to the course modules: creating educational infographics, establishing a professional digital footprint, practicing collaborative coding, mastering email etiquette, and researching cybercrime prevention. By organizing these deliverables within a structured GitHub repository, I have not only practiced version control but also built a foundational asset that will support my career growth over the next four years at VIT Bhopal University.
