**Project Report Reflection**

Research Insights:

While researching this project, I was most surprised by the psychological manipulation behind cybercrime. It isn't just about technical hacking; it’s about social engineering. Fraudsters create a high-pressure environment, like "the offer expires in 5 minutes," to force victims to act before they think. I was also shocked to see how rapidly UPI fraud has evolved in India, with scammers now even using "tampered QR codes" at physical shops. 

Habit Change:

As a result of this research, I will personally change my habit of reusing passwords. I am now using a password manager to ensure every account has a unique, complex password, and I have enabled Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on my primary email and banking apps to prevent unauthorized access even if my password is leaked.
