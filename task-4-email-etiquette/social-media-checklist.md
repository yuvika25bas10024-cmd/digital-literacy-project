Social Media Responsibility Checklist

  **Do's**
- Verify news or "circulars" from official college handles before sharing.	
- Set your profiles to "Private" to control who sees your personal life.	
- Ask for permission before posting photos or videos of your friends/peers.	
- Use professional platforms like LinkedIn to build a positive digital footprint.	
- Report any instance of cyberbullying or harassment to the platform immediately.

**Don'ts**
- Don't post your ID card, Aadhaar, or hall ticket online (Doxxing risk).
- Don't engage with "trolls" or use offensive language in comment sections.
- Don't click on "Free Giveaway" or "Gift" links sent via DM/WhatsApp.
- Don't vent about professors or your college using identifying names.
- Don't accept follow requests from completely anonymous or "bot" accounts.
